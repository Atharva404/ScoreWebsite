SCOR APP

Welcome to the Scor App that lets you make goals and compete with your friends to break bad habits! The purpose of this app is to be encouraged to take the challenge of completing goals within your community.

Check out our github code for the app!

What is our App?

Why

To bring the community together. You can compete with your friends to complete tasks! The motivation is endless!

Purpose

To complete tasks and break bad habits. By using our app, you set your desired goals and attempt to conquer your goals!

Reason

Make a better version of you! By completing tasks, you are already making a better version of you!
