SCOR APP

Welcome to the Scor App that lets you make goals and compete with your friends to break bad habits! The purpose of this app is to be encouraged to take the challenge of completing goals within your community.

What is our App?

Why

To bring the community together. You can compete with your friends to complete tasks! The motivation is endless!

Purpose

To complete tasks and break bad habits. By using our app, you set your desired goals and attempt to conquer your goals!

Reason

Make a better version of you! By completing tasks, you are already making a better version of you!

Features

What does the app offer?

  • Users can enter their tasks that they want to complete
  • All the goals that the user has created will be presented in the home view
  • The app is very user friendly! It is very easy to create tasks
  • You can compete with your friends to complete the most number of tasks!
  • As the user makes a task, the user is given 5 points, which is shown in their profile
  • All the data is stored using Firebase, that way users can log in without making a new account!
  • Users can set their location, target, desired score, as well as a description, to motivate themselves to complete the goal!
